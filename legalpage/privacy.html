Introduction

Welcome to Sarkari Tools. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

By accessing or using JanSeva Kendra Tools, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the website immediately.

Important Notice: We DO NOT store any of your documents, ID cards, images, or files on our servers. All processing (PDF tools, ID resize, image tools, converters, etc.) happens locally in your browser, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

You may voluntarily provide personal information when you:

  • Contact us through the contact form
  • Subscribe to newsletters or updates
  • Send support requests
  • Submit feedback or suggestions

This may include:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your message or query

We ONLY use this information to respond to your requests or provide support.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit JanSeva Kendra Tools, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address (approximate location)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the site
  • Referring page
  • Device type (mobile/desktop)

This information is used solely for analytics and improving website performance.

Files and Documents

JanSeva Kendra Tools provides document-related tools such as:

  • ID card resizing (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, etc.)
  • Image-to-PDF converter
  • PDF merge, compress, convert tools
  • Photo & signature tools
  • CSC utility tools

For all such tools:

  • Files are processed locally in your browser
  • No files are stored on our servers
  • No Aadhaar number, PAN number, or personal document data is stored
  • No content is analyzed, scanned, or shared
  • Any temporary processing files are auto-deleted instantly
